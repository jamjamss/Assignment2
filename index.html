About Me

My name is Nadirah and I work in the finance department for Fairview Hospital. I deal primarily with health insurances. Verifying coverages, reading EOB's, submitting claims, and etc.,. I have been in my current position for the past year and a half. I enjoy what I do and I find myself being resourcesful to my community. I am a first generation college student and I am also the oldest of 6 children. I have studied Public Health at the University of St Thomas University.

I came to America when I was 3 years old. I speak two languages and I have visited Somalia twice since moving to America. I plan on completing my coding certificate and hopefully getting a position in front and back end software development.
